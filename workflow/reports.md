Visual spending breakdown — donut chart by category, bar chart over time, payer split. The analysis view.

## User Stories

- As Karen, I want to see how much I spent per category this month so I know where the money went.
- As a household, I want to see who spent how much so we have full transparency.

## Success

- Monthly spending by category (donut chart)
- Spending over time (bar chart, monthly)
- Payer breakdown (Karen vs husband)
- Date range selector
- Category drill-down: click a category to see its expenses

### Out of Scope

Custom report builder. Export to PDF. Budget targets.
